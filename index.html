1 About

UnScrapped is a movement towards more sustainable fashion practices. There is such a craze around getting new clothes when people don't realize the power of their current wardrobe. This website aims to address people's desires for a new wardrobe by using what they have. Not so much out with the old and in with the new, but rather make do with what you have to make it new!

In this website, there are forums on what to do with old clothes, an online thrift shop, and additional resources on how to be fashionable and sustainable!
